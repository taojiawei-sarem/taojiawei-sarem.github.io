Biography

I am currently a direct Ph.D. student in Electronic Science and Technology at Nanjing University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Mengmeng Li, Prof. Ying Zhang, and Prof. Dazhi Ding. I received my B.Eng. degree in Microelectronic Science and Engineering from Nanjing University of Science and Technology in 2025.

My research interests include synthetic aperture radar (SAR) jamming, radar signal processing, electromagnetic scattering modeling, scattering-center analysis, and physics-inspired deep learning for radar and remote sensing.

Interests

In addition to radar signal processing and electromagnetic modeling, I am also interested in FPGA-based hardware development and real-time signal processing systems. I have worked on video stream scaling based on Efinix FPGA platforms, where bilinear interpolation was implemented for arbitrary-ratio video scaling from a PC to a display device.
